📱 Bedrock Edition

  1. Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition.
  2. Go to Play → Servers.
  3. Select Add Server.
  4. Use address play.wilden.fun and port 19132.
  5. Save and join.

💻 Java Edition

  1. Open Minecraft Java Edition.
  2. Go to Multiplayer.
  3. Click Add Server.
  4. Use server address play.wilden.fun.
  5. Join the server.

🏡 Land Claims

Land Claims allow players to protect builds, items, and progress. Everything inside a claim is protected and only accessible to players you trust.

🚀 Getting Started

  • Hold a golden shovel.
  • Right-click a block to set the first corner.
  • Right-click another block to set the second corner.
  • Your land is now protected.

📦 Claim Blocks

  • Earn 125 claim blocks per hour of playtime.
  • Earn 125 claim blocks per vote.
  • Buy blocks with /buyclaimblocks at $25 per block.
  • Claim blocks are fully refunded when resizing or deleting a claim.

⚙️ Useful Commands

/claimsguideView claim help.

🌿 Branches

Branches is Wilden's custom progression system. When first joining, players choose a branch with unique passive perks and unlockable upgrades.

🌊 Tides

Built for water players.

  • 20% faster swim speed
  • Water breathing
  • Underwater visibility
  • Bonus fishing luck

🌿 Roots

Built for nature biomes.

  • 15% faster crop growth
  • Natural biome ore chances

🔥 Ember

Built for combat players.

  • Fire aura
  • 50% fire damage reduction
  • 30% Fire Aspect chance

🌾 Verdant

Built for farmers and miners.

  • 10% faster crop growth
  • Extra crop drops
  • Extra ore drop chance

🔄 Switching Branches

Use /branches switch. The first switch is free. The second costs $100,000, and the cost doubles with each switch. All upgrades are permanently lost and non-refundable when switching.

Use /fish menu to open your main fishing hub.

🐟 Fish Shop

Use /fish shop to sell custom fish.

📦 Deliveries

Use /fish deliveries to complete fishing tasks for rewards.

📖 Fish Codex

⚖️ Fish Scales

🎒 Fish Bag

✨ Augments

Use /fish augments to view recipes and /fish augment to apply them.

Build and activate the totem to unlock powerful fishing upgrades.

📈 AuraSkills

AuraSkills rewards players for playing naturally. Actions like mining, farming, and combat give XP, level up skills, and unlock rewards.

🧠 Skills & XP

  • Mining XP from mining.
  • Farming XP from farming.
  • Combat XP from fighting.

📊 Viewing Skills

Use /skills to open the main skill menu. Skill-specific commands like /mining, /farming, and /combat can show individual skill details.

🎁 Rewards

Rewards can include stat increases, commands, items, money, health, mana, wisdom, strength, and other bonuses.

⚙️ Commands Summary

  • /skills — Open main skill menu.
  • /mining, /farming, /combat — View specific skills.
  • /stats — View stats if enabled.
  • /skillpoints — Manage stat points if enabled.

👑 Custom Events System

Wilden features a unique 48-hour event cycle where the community works together to influence what happens on the server.

🟢 Good Events

75% chance. The community contributes gems to extend the duration.

🔴 Bad Events

25% chance. The community contributes gems to stop the event early.

🧙 Wizard NPC

Visit the Wizard at spawn to view timers, contribute gems, view leaderboards, and speed up countdowns.

✨ Good Events

  • Golden Hour — Ore drops doubled.
  • Bountiful Harvest — Mob drops doubled.
  • Fertile Rain — Crops grow 50% faster.
  • Wildlife Invasion — Passive animals spawn in the wild.
  • Lucky Seed — Next crop harvested gives a rare item.

⚠️ Bad Events

  • Frostbite — Cold biome damage.
  • Hunger Curse — Hunger drains faster.
  • Toxic Rain — Players outside take damage.

⚡ Speed-Up Costs

1st use: 100 gems • 2nd: 200 gems • 3rd: 400 gems • 4th: 800 gems • Max: 8x speed-up. Speed-ups are not refunded.

⚖️ Wilden Server Rules

  • Play fairly. Cheats, hacked clients, and unfair mods are not allowed.
  • Do not abuse bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics.
  • Punishment evasion and alternate accounts are prohibited.
  • Follow staff instructions.
  • Treat players with respect.
  • Keep chat appropriate and family-friendly.
  • No X-ray, macros, autoclickers, bots, scripts, or automation.
  • No auto-fishing or unfair AFK farming methods.
  • No duplication glitches or exploit abuse.
  • No spamming or advertising.
  • Do not create lag machines or crash attempts.
  • Do not interfere with protected areas.
  • Skins and usernames must be appropriate.

💬 Discord Rules

  • Follow Discord Terms of Service.
  • Do not ping staff unless necessary.
  • Be respectful.
  • No racism, discrimination, hate speech, or harassment.
  • Avoid NSFW, self-harm, drugs, or controversial topics.
  • No spamming, flooding, advertising, scamming, phishing, or trolling.
  • Keep usernames, avatars, and statuses appropriate.
  • Do not share personal information.
  • No threats of DDoS, doxxing, or raids.
  • Do not use alt accounts to avoid punishments.
Rules are subject to change with or without notice.
